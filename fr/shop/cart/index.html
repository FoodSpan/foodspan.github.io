Delivers to your Home

After getting your awesome FoodSpan purchase, we'll ship it straight to your address! If you live in Canada, we'll ship with Canada Post for a nominal fee. It'll be at your house within 9 business days. If you live outside of Canada, it'll cost a bit more and you'll have to pay for customs: we can't guarantee a time. That being said, we're working on connecting FoodSpan to countries all over the world!

Refunds and Warranty

We want to make sure that FoodSpan is an amazing experience for you, but if something goes wrong, we want to help. If you're not satisfied with our product, you can return it and receive a refund on your purchase; and if something breaks or goes wrong within six months of your purchase, we're happy to replace it. Made With Love

All of our products are made with the blood, sweat, and tears of joy from our FoodSpan team. We've put our 100% into making everything here. We're dedicated towards making a dent in the behemoth of food insecurity, with FoodSpan. Hopefully you'll love it as much as we do.

Warranty and Refund Policy

FoodSpan will offer a thirty-day return policy, allowing customers to mail back their unused purchase within thirty days after receiving the shipment and at their own expense in order to receive a full refund. We will have a limited warranty policy allowing for a refund or replacement of the components of a FoodSpan product if they display signs of defection due to workmanship in the six months prior to purchase; this warranty does not cover any problems due to damage inflicted upon the products, and transportation will be paid for by FoodSpan in these instances.
