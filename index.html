FoodSpan

FoodSpan works in your kitchen to help you save food, time, and money, simply by swiping and attaching a tag. Not only does your leftover spaghetti not become a science experiment, but you can save hundreds of dollars every year! Plus, FoodSpan is always available using our suite of web and mobile applications, so you always know what's fresh in your fridge.

The FoodSpan Core

FoodSpan features two core aspects: a Control Panel, and a Tag. Using FoodSpan is simple: just attach the tag to your food container, swipe it past your Control Panel, and click a category button. Then, the Control Panel alerts you to how long food has been in the fridge, and through our companion apps, can tell you when the food is going to expire!
